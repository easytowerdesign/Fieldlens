Field Lens
Purpose
Field Lens is a mobile inspection photo reporting tool. Capture photos during field inspections, add observations, annotate with markup tools, and compile everything into a professional PDF or Word report — ready for submission.
How to Use
1. Create a new report from the home screen
2. Tap Capture to take photos or select from gallery
3. In Photos tab, add captions and observations to each photo
4. Use the toolbar on each photo to:
⊞ Toggle full page (larger photo in report)
↑↓ Reorder photos
↻ Rotate photo
✎ Markup — draw, arrows, circles, text
🗑 Delete photo
5. Go to Export tab, fill in project details
6. Tap Preview to review, or Export to share as PDF or Word
Report Layout
By default, photos are paired 2 per page. Mark any photo as "Full Page" for a larger view — useful for detail shots that need zoom. Landscape photos are automatically sized to 6" wide.
Data Storage
All photos and reports are saved locally on your device. No account or internet required. Photos are also saved to your iPhone Photos library.
